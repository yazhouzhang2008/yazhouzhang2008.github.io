About Me

I am currently a Lecturer at Henan Key Laboratory of Food Safety Data Intelligence, College of Software Engineering, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry. I received the B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees in computer science from Harbin University of Science and Technology, in 2012 and 2015 respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Tianjin University, in 2020. I was a visiting researcher at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2013, Beijing Institute of Technology in 2019, and University of Macau in 2019. I served as the reviewer for NLP major conferences, e.g., SIGIR, ACL, EMNLP, NLPCC, etc.
In recent five years, I have published about 20 papers, including CCF ranking A/B conference papers (e.g., IJCAI, CIKM, IEEE BIBM, etc.) and high-quality journal papers (e.g., Information Fusion, Theoretical Computer Science, Frontiers in Neuroscience, etc.).
